All data are preliminary and may change as we receive more information. Some data on these pages lack information on demographic characteristics like race and ethnicity. The NYC Health Department acknowledges this lack of representation in the data is inequitable, and we are committed to developing methods for ensuring more inclusive, equitable representation in the future. [Learn more about health inequities in data](https://www.nyc.gov/assets/doh/respiratory-illness-data/index.html#/about#inequities).
