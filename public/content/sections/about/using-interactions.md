#### Enlarging a chart

**Click or tap** any stat card or chart to open a larger version in a modal. Press **Escape** or click outside the modal to close it.

#### Visits vs. hospitalizations

On the Emergency Department data type, a toggle at the top of the stat cards switches between:

- **Visits** — percent of all ED visits resulting in a diagnosis of the illness.
- **Hospitalizations** — percent of ED visits resulting in inpatient admission.

Hospitalizations are a subset of visits and show lower percentages. The toggle updates all cards simultaneously.

#### Hovering charts

Hovering over a chart line reveals a **tooltip** with the exact date and value. On the neighborhood map, hovering shows the neighborhood name and case rate.

#### Filtering by demographics

Where available, a **group selector** above the chart lets you filter by age group, sex, or race/ethnicity. The selector only appears when demographic data is available for that metric.

> Not all data sources include demographic breakdowns — ED visit data in particular often lacks race/ethnicity information.

#### Sharing a deep link

The browser URL updates as you scroll and switch data types. Copy it at any point to share a link that lands someone directly on the same section and view you're looking at.
