The Respiratory Illness Data Pages show data on [COVID-19](https://www.nyc.gov/site/doh/covid/covid-19-main.page), [flu (influenza)](https://www.nyc.gov/site/doh/health/health-topics/flu-seasonal.page), and [RSV (respiratory syncytial virus)](https://www.nyc.gov/site/doh/health/health-topics/respiratory-syncytial-virus.page) in New York City collected by the NYC Health Department.
